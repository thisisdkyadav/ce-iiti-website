About the Programme

Engineering India's
Mobility Future

India's National Infrastructure Pipeline targets ₹111 lakh crore in transportation investment. Despite this massive scale, there is a critical shortage of skilled professionals. This programme bridges that gap.

Research Focus

Areas of Specialisation

Four interconnected research domains, with active collaborations with MoRTH, NHAI, and State PWDs across India.

01
Traffic Engineering, ITS & Road Safety
Traffic flow theory, microscopic & macroscopic modelling, signal design, intelligent transportation systems, road safety audits, crash data analysis, and Vision Zero methodologies.
Traffic FlowSignal DesignITSRoad SafetySimulation
02
Pavement Engineering & Materials
Bituminous mix design, pavement structural analysis and design, performance characterisation, reclaimed asphalt pavement (RAP), and sustainable construction materials.
Mix DesignStructural DesignRAPRheology
03
Transportation Planning & Logistics
Travel demand modelling, multimodal network planning, urban freight & logistics, transport economics, land use–transport interaction, and sustainable mobility planning.
Demand ModellingFreightMultimodalUrban Mobility
04
Geometric Design & Sustainable Construction
Highway geometric design, horizontal & vertical alignment, interchange design, pedestrian & bicycle infrastructure, and environmentally responsive road construction.
Alignment DesignInterchangesNMTIRC/AASHTO

Admission Criteria

🎓

Educational Qualification

Four-year B.E./B.Tech. in Civil Engineering with First Division

  • Min. 60% marks or CGPA 6.0/10 for General / OBC / EWS
  • Min. 55% marks or CGPA 5.5/10 for SC / ST / PwD
  • International applicants: equivalent qualification assessed by IIT Indore
📝

Qualifying Examination

Valid GATE score in Civil Engineering (CE) — mandatory for Indian students

  • COAP 2026 registration required for TA seat consideration
  • International students: valid TOEFL or IELTS + GRE score

How to Apply

  1. 1
    Qualify GATE CEObtain a valid GATE Civil Engineering score — the mandatory entry qualification for Indian students.
  2. 2
    Register on COAPVisit coap2026.iitr.ac.in and register for centralised PG admissions (typically April–May).
  3. 3
    Apply on IIT Indore PortalFill the online form, upload GATE scorecard, degree certificates, and mark sheets.
  4. 4
    Written Test & InterviewShortlisted candidates attend evaluation at IIT Indore campus.
  5. 5
    Accept Offer & JoinMerit list based on GATE score, academics, and interview. Confirm your seat to join.
